The bet

The web rents you space and keeps your work.

Amber is the opposite bet. Markdown on your own disk, hand-editable config, AGPL. You can leave anytime — because the files were always yours.

Themes

One space. Any look.

A theme is just CSS and HTML. Switch the whole look without touching a word of your content.
