# Amber — Logo Prompts (Gem direction)

For **Gemini 2.5 Flash Image / Nano Banana Pro** in the Gemini app or AI Studio.
Reference: the **Obsidian app logo** — a faceted crystal with dimension, internal
facet planes, and depth — but rendered as a **warm amber gemstone**.

## The core idea
Amber is a gemstone: fossil resin, polished into jewels, **translucent and glowing
from within**. Where Obsidian faceted volcanic glass, Amber facets the amber gem.
The glow is the magic — amber lights up when light passes through it. That's the
distinctive, iconic quality we want; the earlier flat marks threw it away.

## Constraints
- A **single faceted / polished amber gemstone**, dimensional and jewel-like.
- **Translucent, glowing from within** — warm internal light.
- Honey-amber gradient across facets: bright **#E6BA60** highlights → signature
  **#C6871F** → deep **#9A6314 / #5A3809** in shadow.
- Premium and calm (like Obsidian), not glossy/cheesy. Subtle, not neon.
- **Single iconic mark, centered, on a transparent or flat background.**
- Must still read at favicon size — keep the silhouette simple even if the facets
  are detailed.
- **Not** a literal resin drip or a bug-in-amber.

## How to use
1. Paste a prompt → ask for **4 variations**.
2. Refine conversationally: "fewer, bigger facets", "stronger inner glow", "sharper
   silhouette", "less glossy", "more translucent", "center it on transparent bg".
3. Generate the **gem alone first**, no text. Wordmark/lockup after.
4. Bring 2–3 favorites back here → we refine and rebuild as a clean SVG (Obsidian's
   logo is an SVG with gradient facets; we can do the same).

## Direction A — Faceted cut gem (closest to Obsidian)
> A modern app-icon logo: a single faceted amber gemstone, cut like a crystal with
> clean geometric facet planes, viewed three-quarter so it reads dimensional.
> Translucent and glowing warmly from within. Honey-amber gradient — bright #E6BA60
> highlights on top facets, signature #C6871F mid, deep #9A6314 in the lower facets.
> Soft light catching the facet edges. Premium, calm, jewel-like — in the spirit of
> the Obsidian app logo but in warm amber. Centered on a transparent background, no
> text, no busy reflections. Reads clearly at small sizes.

## Direction B — Polished cabochon with inner glow (softest, most "Amber")
> A modern app-icon logo: a single smooth, rounded amber cabochon gemstone — domed and
> polished — translucent and lit warmly from within, as if a soft light sits behind it.
> A gentle gradient from a bright #E6BA60 glowing core out to deep #9A6314 edges, with
> one soft highlight. Warm, calm, premium, tactile. Centered on a transparent
> background, no text, minimal, iconic at favicon size.

## Direction C — Crystal shard (most distinctive silhouette)
> A modern logo mark: a single upright amber crystal shard with a few bold faceted
> planes, dimensional and translucent, glowing warmly from inside. Sharp, confident
> silhouette; honey-amber gradient from bright #E6BA60 at the lit edge to deep #5A3809
> in shadow. Premium and quiet like the Obsidian logo, in warm amber. Centered,
> transparent background, no text.

## Direction D — Faceted gem shaped as an "A"
> A modern app-icon logo: a faceted amber gemstone whose overall silhouette suggests a
> soft geometric letter "A". Clean facet planes, dimensional, translucent and glowing
> from within. Honey-amber gradient (#E6BA60 highlights, #C6871F body, #9A6314 shadow).
> Premium and calm, Obsidian-logo spirit in warm amber. Centered, transparent
> background, no separate text, reads at favicon size.

## Direction E — Light through amber (glow-forward)
> A modern logo mark: a faceted amber gemstone with strong backlight passing through it,
> so it glows like stained glass — bright translucent #E6BA60 core radiating into
> #C6871F and #9A6314 facets. A few clean geometric facet planes give it structure.
> Warm, luminous, premium, calm. Single centered gem on a transparent background, no
> text, simple silhouette.

## Wordmark / lockup (AFTER picking the gem)
> A clean wordmark of the word "amber" in lowercase, friendly rounded geometric
> sans-serif, deep warm ink #2A2622 on transparent background. Even spacing, no effects.
> Then lock it up next to [the chosen amber gem], gem on the left, wordmark centered.

## Nano Banana Pro tips
- It **edits** — refine the same gem ("bigger facets", "warmer glow") rather than re-rolling.
- Say **"transparent background", "app icon", "centered"** to get a clean, usable mark.
- Push **"translucent / glowing from within"** hard — that's what separates an amber gem
  from a generic orange crystal.
- If it gets glossy/3D-render-y, add **"flat lighting, matte, subtle, not glossy"**.
- Raster only — final logo is a traced/rebuilt SVG with gradient facets.
