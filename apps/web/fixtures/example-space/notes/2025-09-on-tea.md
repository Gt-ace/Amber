I bought a small kyusu in February and it has reorganized my mornings. Not
dramatically — I still wake up too late and answer email before I should — but
the first hour of the day now belongs to a pot of sencha and whatever book I'm
reading, which is a better arrangement than what came before.

The trick, if there is one, is that the tea takes long enough to brew that I
can't justify checking my phone in the meantime. Two minutes is too short to
start anything and too long to stare at the kettle. So I read.
