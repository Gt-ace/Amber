I trained as a printmaker and slid sideways into editorial illustration around
2017. These days I split my time between client work — mostly book covers and
the occasional magazine spread — and a slow rotation of personal projects.

Before all that I wrote backend code for a logistics company in Rotterdam,
which is why my websites tend to be plainer than my drawings.
